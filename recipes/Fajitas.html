Fajitas

A recipe for chicken fajitas

Ingredients

  1. 1 tablespoon: Worcestershire sauce, cider vinegar, soy sauce
  2. 1 teaspoon chilli powder
  3. 1 clove of garlic minced
  4. 1 dash of hot pepper sauce
  5. 1 1/2 pounds chicken thighs cut into strips
  6. 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  7. 1 onion, thinly sliced
  8. 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  9. 1/2 lemon, juiced

Directions

Step 1

Combine Worcestershire sauce, vinegar, soy sauce, chili powder, garlic, and hot pepper sauce in a medium bowl. Place chicken in sauce, turning to coat. Marinate at room temperature for 30 minutes or cover and refrigerate for several hours.

Step 2

Heat oil in a large skillet over high heat. Add chicken strips; cook and stir in hot oil for 5 minutes. Add onion and green pepper; saut√© for 3 minutes. Remove from heat and sprinkle with lemon juice.
