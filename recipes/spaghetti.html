Spaghetti

Ingredients

  1. Spaghetti:1 ounce per serving.
  2. Beeflean ground beed, or ground turkey
  3. Butter Use a stick of slice butter to prepare the pan and to add richness and flavour to the baked spaghetti
  4. Cheese shreeded Cheddar Cheese

Directions

Step 1

Boil the spaghetti in salted water, then drain and set aside.

Step 2

Cook the beef on the stove and sicard the grease. Transfer to a bowl and mix in the spaghetti sauce

Step 3

Prepare the backing dish, then spread half of the noodles in the bottom.

Step 4

Pour the beef mizture over the spaghetti and spread in an even layer.

Step 5

Bake for 30 minutes, top with Cheese and continue baking until the cheese is metled.
