Caprese Stuffed Avocado

This Caprese stuffed avocado salad is made up of avocado halves, filled with a simple Caprese salad using cherry tomatoes, mozzerlla pearls, and basil, and drizzled with balsamic vinegar.

Ingredients

  1. 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and cut in half
  2. 1/4 cup of fresh mozzerlla pearls
  3. 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  4. 6 fresh basil leaves, thinly sliced
  5. 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  6. salt and pepper to taste

Directions

Step 1

Arrange avocado halves onto a plate

Step 2

Toss together mozzerella pearls, cherry tomatoes, and basil in a small bowl. Spoon the Caprese filling into each avocado half and drizzle with balsamic vinegar. Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.
